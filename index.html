About the Workshop

QKD Based
Fiber Connect 2026

We are excited to announce QKD Based Fiber Connect 2026, a premier one-day workshop dedicated to exploring the latest advancements and innovations in the field of quantum communication using optical fiber technology.

This workshop aims to convene leading experts, researchers, and practitioners to delve into the newest developments and applications in Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and related quantum communication technologies.

Netaji Subhas University of Technology (formerly Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology) is regarded as one of the premier engineering institutions in India. Established in 1983 as Delhi Institute of Technology, NSUT has consistently been ranked as one of the top engineering colleges in India.

🔬 Cutting-Edge Research

Covering both theoretical foundations and experimental demonstrations of QKD systems, including live Coherent One-Way QKD demonstrations.

🤝 Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Bringing together researchers, academics, policymakers, and industry professionals to foster collaboration and advance quantum communication.

🏛️ Distinguished Faculty

Led by experts from NSUT, IIT Indore, and ERNET India with deep expertise in quantum technologies, photonic networks, and signal processing.

Programme Content

Topics & Demonstrations

🔗

Physical Layer Modeling for QKD Networks

Fiber loss, dispersion, and noise models for real-world QKD channel characterisation and link-budget analysis.

⚙️

Resource Allocation Strategies in Quantum Networks

Optimal wavelength assignment, key-rate aware scheduling, and capacity planning for multi-user QKD networks.

⚡

Dynamic Power Allocation in QKD Systems

Adaptive power control techniques to mitigate Raman noise and optimise QKD performance in DWDM environments.

🗺️

Advanced QKD Routing Techniques

Survivable routing algorithms, disaster-resilient path selection, and SDN-driven control planes for QKD optical networks.

🛡️

Trusted Relay Node (TRN) Placement Optimization

Strategic TRN placement for extending QKD reach, ensuring key continuity, and minimising network vulnerability.

🔓

Various Security Attacks on QKD

Photon-number splitting, intercept-resend, and side-channel attacks — detection strategies and countermeasures.

🤖

Machine Learning-Based QKD Optimization

Deep reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, and LSTM-based proactive routing for intelligent QKD networks.

🔭

Live Demonstration

Experience live quantum-secure key generation with our Coherent One-Way (COW) QKD system. Witness real-time QBER monitoring, key distillation, and privacy amplification on actual quantum hardware.
